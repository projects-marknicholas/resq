How It Works

A seamless flow from incident reporting to resolution

1

Report Incident

Citizens report emergencies through the mobile app with GPS location and photos

2

Barangay Response

Local barangay receives alert first and responds if capable, or escalates to dispatcher

3

Deploy Resources

Dispatcher assigns nearest available units from BFP, DOH, PNP, or LGU agencies

4

Track & Resolve

Real-time tracking of response units until incident is resolved and feedback collected
